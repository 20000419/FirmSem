# Frozen Result Scope

The public release separates three empirical strata:

- Development: 131 independently reviewed obligations, with 129 binary-scored
  rows after retaining two ambiguous labels outside the binary denominator.
- Project-disjoint holdout: 37 eligible scored obligations from five projects
  absent from development, including five eliminated checks.
- Mechanism challenge: 16 scored build rows in eight real-incident pairs after
  fresh 2+1 review excluded four complete non-security-check pairs.

The controlled compiler replay demonstrates that standards-conforming
optimization can change a security-policy outcome and produce an
attacker-controlled overwrite. It does not estimate field prevalence or claim
that every observed guard loss is optimizer-caused.

Assembly-only inference remains an identifiability stress test. Source-backed
obligation verification is the supported audit setting. See
`annotation_protocol.md` and the frozen JSON summaries under `data/` for exact
denominators and agreement statistics.
